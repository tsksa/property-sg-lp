Honest take

My read on this one.

Newport Residences is built for a specific buyer — the executive or expat who values minutes over dollars and wants the CBD lifestyle without the commute. It's not the right fit for suburban upgraders looking for space, nor for first-time buyers priced out of the tier-1 entry point. If you're in the target segment, the mixed-use setup and CDL brand give it resilience that standalone luxury condos in the same area lack.
