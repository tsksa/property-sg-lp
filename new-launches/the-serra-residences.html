Why The Serra

What makes this one work.

Freehold in the CCR

Freehold tenure in Novena — rare. Most new launches in this corridor are 99-year leasehold. Direct resale-value implications.

Medical hub proximity

Mount Elizabeth Novena, Tan Tock Seng, Novena Medical Centre all within walking distance. Strong rental pool from healthcare professionals + medical tourists.

Top-tier school zones

Within proximity catchments for ACS (Barker Road), CHIJ Primary (Toa Payoh), and accessible to Raffles Girls. Premium school-priority buyers.

MRT connectivity

Novena MRT (North-South Line) directly to Orchard, Dhoby Ghaut, and CBD. One-stop to Newton interchange for Downtown Line access.

Honest take

My read on this one.

The Serra works if you're buying for long-term family living or medical-professional rental yield. Freehold tenure in D11 is the real moat — leasehold condos nearby face value decay after year 20, this one doesn't. Downside is size: as a boutique development, you won't get the full facility scale of a mega-project. For buyers who value Novena's walkability, medical access, and freehold resale protection more than pool-gym scale, this is a strong fit. Get in early on VIP preview if you want corner stacks facing east or park views.
