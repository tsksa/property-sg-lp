Why The Serra

What makes this one work.

Freehold in the CCR

Freehold tenure in Novena — rare. Most new launches in this corridor are 99-year leasehold. Direct resale-value implications.

Medical hub proximity

Mount Elizabeth Novena, Tan Tock Seng, Novena Medical Centre all within walking distance. Strong rental pool from healthcare professionals + medical tourists.

Top-tier school zones

Within proximity catchments for ACS (Barker Road), CHIJ Primary (Toa Payoh), and accessible to Raffles Girls. Premium school-priority buyers.

MRT connectivity

Novena MRT (North-South Line) directly to Orchard, Dhoby Ghaut, and CBD. One-stop to Newton interchange for Downtown Line access.
