Why Faber Residence

What makes this one work.

Tri-JV build quality

GuocoLand, Hong Leong Holdings, and TID — three of Singapore's most established residential developers building jointly. Historically, tri-JV projects achieve above-market build quality with strong resale track records.

One-north catchment

Walking proximity to one-north innovation hub, NUS, Fusionopolis, Biopolis, Mediapolis. Strong rental pool from researchers, professionals, and NUS postgraduate students.

NUH & medical-rental

National University Hospital directly accessible — a significant rental demand source for medical professionals and patient families relocating for treatment.

MRT connectivity

Clementi (East-West Line) and Dover MRT within proximity. Direct access to CBD, one-north, Jurong East, and the future Cross Island Line junction at Clementi.

Honest take

My read on this one.

Faber Residence is a professional-catchment play. The 2BR and 3BR Basic units are perfectly sized for young researchers, medical professionals at NUH, and one-north workers — very strong rental yield thesis. 4BR and 5BR are genuine family units for upgraders who want D05 proximity without Bukit Timah pricing.

The three-developer JV is a quality signal: tri-party projects tend to avoid cost-cutting that single-developer projects sometimes show. Not the right fit for buyers wanting CCR prestige or OCR price points — D05 sits in the middle in both dimensions.
