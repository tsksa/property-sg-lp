Why Zyon Grand

The Kim Seng positioning case.

CBD-fringe connectivity

D03 sits between Orchard CCR and Alexandra/Redhill RCR — ideal for working professionals. Great World MRT walk + Redhill EW15 easy access = central connectivity without CCR pricing.

CDL pedigree

City Developments Limited — Singapore's largest listed property developer. Track record on build quality, after-sales, and CCR fringe positioning via projects like Piccadilly Grand and Orchard Sophia.

4BR with Private Lift

Private-lift 4BR units at 1,421sqft are a rarity at this price point in the CCR-fringe. Signals ultra-premium positioning within the development.

Singapore River lifestyle belt

Walking distance to Robertson Quay, Clarke Quay, and the Singapore River F&B + entertainment corridor. Orchard Road shopping within 10 minutes.
