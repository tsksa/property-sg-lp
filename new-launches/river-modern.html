Why River Modern

The CCR case for this one.

Prime D09 CCR positioning

District 9 is one of the three CCR (Core Central Region) districts. Historically most resilient in capital appreciation and rental demand across property cycles.

Great World MRT connectivity

Great World MRT (Thomson-East Coast Line) within walking distance. Direct to Orchard, Outram Park, and the entire TEL corridor. No car required for CBD or Orchard commutes.

Singapore River F&B & lifestyle belt

Robertson Quay, Clarke Quay, and the Singapore River promenade within 10 minutes walk. Amenity density few CCR projects match — permanent riverside character.

GuocoLand track record

GuocoLand's CCR projects (Wallich Residences, Martin Modern, Meyer Mansion) consistently achieve strong build quality and resale performance. Pedigree developer with CCR specialisation.

Honest take

My read on this one.

River Modern is a pedigree CCR play. 2BR Plus at 646sqft is the sweet-spot for rental-yield investors — Great World MRT + F&B belt = strong expat/young-professional rental demand. 3BR Flex and 3BR Plus work for families who want to live in CCR without stretching to D10/D11 freehold pricing (99-year here vs freehold elsewhere).

4BR Plus at 1,830sqft is for genuine family CCR buyers — limited supply at this size in D09, early registration matters. Not right for buyers wanting OCR entry pricing or suburban space-per-dollar — this is premium CCR and priced accordingly.

— Joe Tay, District Director at ERA Realty Network · CEA R009618D
