Why Dunearn House

What this delivers.

Tier-1 school catchment

Within 2km of Nanyang Primary and close to ACS (Barker Road) — two of Singapore's most sought-after primary schools. Priority-1 eligibility for parents living within 1km.

Freehold in District 10

D10 freehold — the highest-prestige tenure in the highest-prestige district. Historical data shows D10 freehold outperforms the broader private market in capital appreciation.

Botanic Gardens proximity

UNESCO World Heritage Botanic Gardens within 10 minutes. Permanent greenery protection means no future high-density development will surround you.

Downtown Line connectivity

Tan Kah Kee or Sixth Avenue MRT (DT Line) direct to Bugis, Bayfront, and Marina Bay. Quick access to CBD for working parents.
