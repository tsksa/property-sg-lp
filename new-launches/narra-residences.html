Why Narra Residences

The Hillview positioning case.

Hillview Nature corridor

Adjacent to Dairy Farm Nature Park, Bukit Timah Nature Reserve, and the Rail Corridor. Permanent green-belt protection means no future high-density development on your horizon.

Hillview MRT (DT3)

Downtown Line direct to Bugis, Bayfront, and Marina Bay. Walking distance means no bus transfer — a meaningful daily-commute differentiator at OCR pricing.

Established estate

D23 Bukit Batok / Bukit Panjang is a mature estate with schools, hawker centres, and shopping (HillV2, Rail Mall, Bukit Panjang Plaza) already in place. Not a construction-site neighbourhood.

Wide unit mix for flexibility

10 distinct layouts means buyers can match a unit to specific needs. 2BR Compact for entry, 3BR Premium for families, 1BR+Study for rental — all under the same build quality umbrella.

Honest take

My read on this one.

Narra Residences is a nature-premium OCR play. D23 Hillview is one of the few mature estates in Singapore with genuine nature adjacency — Dairy Farm, Bukit Timah Nature Reserve, Rail Corridor all permanent. For buyers who value that lifestyle, OCR pricing for a nature-adjacent unit is compelling.

2BR Premium (646sqft) and 3BR Compact (818sqft) are the best-value layouts. 3BR Premium at 990+ works for families prioritising Hillview estate over central locations. Not the right fit for buyers who prioritise city connectivity — Hillview is 20 minutes to town, suburbia lifestyle.
