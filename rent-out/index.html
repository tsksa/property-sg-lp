Before you ask

The landlord questions I hear most.

"How much commission do I pay?"

Half a month's rent for a 1-year lease (industry standard) — and it comes from the tenant, not you. For 2-year leases it's one month's rent, split per standard market practice. No upfront fees.

"What if the tenant defaults?"

Standard 2-month security deposit covers the typical risk. My vetting is designed to minimise this from the start — employment, payment history, and reference checks. If issues arise, I help you handle termination and re-listing.

"How quickly can you find a tenant?"

Average 15 days from listing to signed lease for well-priced HDBs and condos. Faster in high-demand estates (Serangoon, Tampines near MRT). I give you a specific forecast at the free consult.

"Can you help me manage the property long-term?"

Yes. After placement I handle renewal negotiations, ad-hoc landlord requests, and re-letting when the tenant moves out. No retainer fee — pay per transaction.
