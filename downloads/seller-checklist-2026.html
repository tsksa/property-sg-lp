Selling an HDB in Singapore runs on a surprisingly tight sequence — skip a step and the process stalls for weeks. This one-page checklist covers every milestone from pre-listing through key handover, matched to the current 2026 HDB process. Print it, tick off as you go.
Phase 1 — Pre-listing2–4 weeks before
  • Confirm MOP has cleared Log into HDB website → My HDBPage → Flat Details. Verify MOP end date.
  • Check CPF accrued interest CPF website → Statement → Withdrawal Statement. This amount returns to your CPF OA on sale.
  • Get indicative valuation from your agent Built from recent block transactions + SRX X-Value + condition adjustments. Not the same as HDB's formal valuation.
  • Decide asking price and negotiation floor Asking = indicative valuation + 1.5–3% buffer. Floor = the number below which you walk away.
  • Book professional photography 30–40 photos, floorplan annotated. Dusk shots for living areas if possible.
  • Minor repairs / declutter Paint only if walls damaged. Declutter is free and always pays off.
  • Draft listing copy MRT distance in metres, school zones, level, facing direction, recent upgrades.
Phase 2 — Listing launchWeek 0
  • PropertyGuru, 99.co, SRX, EdgeProp launched same day Same listing, same price, coordinated same-day launch maximizes "new listing" boost.
  • Activate premium listing boost PropertyGuru Turbo / 99.co Spotlight. Should be in your agent's 2% package.
  • Block calendar for viewings Evenings + full weekends, next 4 weeks. Don't travel during first 14 days.
  • Agent available on WhatsApp Confirmed response time commitment — your agent should be responsive to buyer agents within 1 hour.
Phase 3 — Active sellingWeeks 1–6
  • Track viewing requests + actual shows Target: 15–40 requests, 5–12 actual viewings in first 4 weeks.
  • Weekly review of feedback from viewings Common objections reveal whether it's pricing, condition, or positioning.
  • Price-drop trigger set If under 5 viewings by week 2 → drop price 3–5%. Don't let listings go stale.
  • Offer log maintained Date, amount, buyer's agent contact, status. Essential if multiple offers come in.
  • Negotiation strategy agreed with agent Counter-offer floor, walkaway conditions, who speaks to whom when.
Phase 4 — Option to Purchase (OTP) signedWeek 4–6
  • Option Fee collected S$1 – S$1,000 negotiated. Not a percentage of sale price.
  • OTP signed with correct dates Option date, exercise deadline (21 days), completion date.
  • Option Exercise deadline diarised 21 calendar days, including weekends. Expires 4pm on day 21.
  • Conveyancing lawyer engaged Typical cost S$1,800–S$3,500. Engage immediately after OTP signed.
  • CPF refund amount recalculated Based on current accrued interest. Your net proceeds = sale price − outstanding loan − CPF refund − commission − legal fees.
Phase 5 — OTP exercised + resale applicationWeek 6–9
  • Option Exercise Fee collected Combined Option Fee + Exercise Fee cannot exceed S$5,000 total.
  • Submit resale application via HDB Resale Portal Admin fee: S$40 (1–2 room) or S$80 (3-room and larger). Both parties submit separately.
  • HDB acknowledgement received Within ~10 working days via SMS and email. This is when the clock starts on your completion date.
Phase 6 — Online document endorsementWeek 9–11
  • SMS from HDB received Confirms documents are ready to endorse on the HDB Resale Portal.
  • Review and endorse all documents online 6-day window from SMS receipt. Both sellers endorse separately if jointly owned.
  • Pay all required fees HDB legal fees (if using in-house) or private conveyancing fees. Stamp duty is buyer's responsibility.
  • Monitor for HDB approval ~2 weeks after full endorsement. No in-person appointment required at this stage.
Phase 7 — Resale CompletionWeek 14–16
  • Completion Appointment scheduled At HDB Hub (Toa Payoh). Approximately 8 weeks after HDB accepts resale application. You'll get SMS with date/time.
  • Meter readings recorded Utilities (SP Group) transferred. Do this at least 3 days before completion.
  • Full move-out 3 days prior All personal belongings removed. Clean the unit. Take final photos.
  • Keys, access cards, HDB items prepared Mailbox keys, gate access cards, HDB-issued items (letter box).
  • Attend Resale Completion Appointment Sign transfer docs, receive sale proceeds via lawyer, hand over keys to buyer.
  • CPF refund received in OA Within ~5 working days of completion. Check CPF account to confirm.
