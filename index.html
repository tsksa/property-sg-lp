How I work

From valuation to closing — a process built for Singapore homeowners.

Whether you're selling your HDB flat in Tampines, renting out a condo in District 10, or pricing a landed home in Bukit Timah, I follow the same four-step process — transparent, dedicated, and built around real Singapore market data (URA, SRX, HDB resale index).

01 Day 1

Free consultation & valuation

A 30-minute call with me, your dedicated CEA-registered advisor. I review your property, local comparables, URA transaction data, and the current Singapore property market to give you an honest price range — no sales pressure, no obligation.

02 Day 2–4

Listing launch

Professional photography, floorplan optimisation, and copy written to highlight what buyers and tenants actually care about. Your listing goes live on PropertyGuru, 99.co, SRX and EdgeProp — plus my private buyer network — within 48 hours.

03 Week 1–3

Qualified viewings & offers

I pre-screen every buyer and tenant — financing verified, intent confirmed — so you only meet serious ones. I handle viewings, negotiation, and counter-offers directly on WhatsApp so you're never in the dark.

04 Week 3–8

OTP to completion

Option to Purchase drafted, conveyancing coordinated with your lawyer, CPF and HDB paperwork handled end-to-end. Just me — from first call to key handover — no handoffs, no scripts, no rotating agents.
